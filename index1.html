Scrambled-Egg

Definition:

Scrambled egg is a dish made from beaten eggs stirred while cooking, typically with a small amount of butter or oil. It can be seasoned with various ingredients such as salt, pepper, cheese, herbs, or vegetables. The eggs are cooked until they are set but still moist, resulting in a soft and creamy texture.

Example Sentence:

"For breakfast, I usually enjoy a plate of scrambled eggs with toast."

Contextual Information:

Scrambled eggs are a popular breakfast food enjoyed around the world and can be served on their own or as part of a larger meal.
